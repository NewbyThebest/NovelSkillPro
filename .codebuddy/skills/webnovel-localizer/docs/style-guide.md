# 语言风格改写规范

> 所有改写级别（A/B/C）都必须遵守本文件的规则。按优先级排列。

---

## 规则1：Deep POV（深度限制视角）

一个场景只从一个角色的视角叙述。读者只能知道POV角色能看到、听到、感受到、想到的东西。不能出现POV角色无法得知的信息。

```
❌ He looked at her coldly. She felt scared. He actually felt heartache inside.
   → 问题：同时展示了他的内心和她的感受，视角混乱

✅ His jaw clenched as he stared her down, willing his expression to stay cold.
   But fuck—the way her eyes glistened nearly broke him.
   → 纯男主视角：他能控制自己的表情，能看到她的眼睛，内心挣扎是他自己的
```

**常见POV错误**：
- 「她不知道他其实很心疼」→ 如果是她的POV，她不可能知道他心疼
- 「他觉得她很美，她感觉到了他的目光」→ 两个人的内心同时出现
- 「整个房间的人都在看他们」→ POV角色不可能同时感知所有人

**解决方案**：改写时先确定本段/本章的POV角色，然后严格只写这个角色能感知的一切。

---

## 规则2：Show, Don't Tell（展示而非告知）

不要直接陈述情感，通过身体反应、动作、内心独白来展示。

```
❌ She was very angry. She wanted to hit him.
✅ Her nails bit into her palms. Every muscle screamed at her to slap that smug look off his face.

❌ He was sad about losing his brother.
✅ His hand found the empty chair at the table. He pulled it out of habit, then froze.

❌ The atmosphere was tense.
✅ The silence in the room had teeth.
```

**Show的三大工具**：
1. **身体反应**：脉搏加速、拳头握紧、呼吸急促、胃部紧缩
2. **微动作**：转移视线、舔嘴唇、手指敲桌子、调整领带
3. **内心独白**：斜体碎片化的心理活动

## 规则3：对话短促有力

海外网文对话要短、punchy、口语化。大量使用缩写（don't, can't, won't, I'm, he's, that's）。

```
❌ "I want you to know that I have already made my decision. No matter what
    anyone says, I will not change my mind. This marriage will proceed as planned."
✅ "The wedding's happening. End of discussion."

❌ "I think that you should understand that in our world, loyalty is the most
    important thing. If you betray the family, there will be consequences."
✅ "Cross the family, and they'll never find your body."

❌ "I do not understand why you are being so difficult about this matter."
✅ "Why are you being such a pain in my ass?"
```

**对话写作要点**：
- 一句对话尽量不超过2行
- 避免角色在对话中「演讲」或「解释」
- 用动作节拍（action beats）代替dialogue tags：
  - ❌ "Get out," he said angrily.
  - ✅ "Get out." He didn't raise his voice. He didn't need to.
- 潜台词比明说更有力：角色不说出真实感受，但读者能感觉到

## 规则4：五感描写

每个场景至少涵盖3种感官。中文网文偏重视觉（外貌），英文网文强调触觉和嗅觉。

**五感清单**：
- **视觉**：光线、颜色、表情细节、空间感
- **听觉**：声音质地、沉默、环境音
- **触觉**：温度、质感、压力、疼痛 ← 英文网文极重要
- **嗅觉**：体味、环境气味、食物 ← 尤其在Werewolf中极重要
- **味觉**：血腥味、酒、泪水的咸味

```
❌ He was very handsome. He had dark hair and deep eyes. He was tall and strong.
   → 纯视觉，而且是最泛化的视觉

✅ The scent of bourbon and something darker—gunpowder, maybe—clung to his
   suit jacket. When his fingers closed around her wrist, his grip was warm,
   almost careful. Nothing like the cold command in his voice.
   → 嗅觉（bourbon+gunpowder）+ 触觉（warm grip）+ 听觉（cold voice）
```

## 规则5：斜体内心独白

英文网文大量使用斜体内心独白。特点：直接、碎片化、情绪化。不是完整的句子，更像意识流。

```
❌ She thought to herself: I should not trust him. He is a dangerous person.
✅ *Don't trust him. Don't you dare trust him.*
   But her traitorous body leaned into his touch anyway.

❌ He thought that she was the most beautiful woman he had ever seen.
✅ *Fuck.* She wasn't supposed to look like that. Not here. Not now.
```

**使用时机**：
- Mate bond触发时（*Mine.*）
- 内心挣扎时（*Walk away. Just walk away.*）
- 震惊/恐惧时（*No. No, no, no.*）
- 自嘲/吐槽时（*Great. Just great.*)

## 规则6：章节节奏公式

每章1200-2000词（视平台调整）。固定四段结构：

### Opening Hook（前50词）
用冲突/悬念/感官冲击立刻抓住读者。第一句话就要有力量。

```
❌ It was a beautiful morning. Elena woke up and looked out the window.
✅ The barrel of a gun pressed against the back of Elena's skull.
   "Don't. Move."

❌ Sera walked into the pack meeting feeling nervous.
✅ Every wolf in the room turned to stare. Sera's wolf whimpered.
   *We shouldn't be here.*
```

### Rising Tension
推进情节的同时持续制造张力。每个段落都要有一个小小的forward momentum。

### Emotional Peak
本章的情感高潮点。可以是一个吻、一次对抗、一个秘密被揭露、一次拒绝。

### Cliffhanger Ending
在最紧张的瞬间断章。让读者必须点开下一章。

```
✅ The door swung open. And standing there, covered in blood that wasn't his own,
   was the one man she'd been running from for three years.
   [END CHAPTER]

✅ "I know what you are," she whispered. "I've always known."
   His blood ran cold.
   [END CHAPTER]
```

## 规则7：翻译腔替换表

以下是中国作者最常出现的「翻译腔」表达及地道替代。**改写时必须逐一检查并替换**。

### 情感与反应

| 翻译腔 | 地道替代 |
|-------|---------|
| She couldn't help but... | She found herself... / Before she knew it... |
| Her heart beat faster | Her pulse spiked / Her heart slammed against her ribs |
| She felt very sad | Something cracked inside her chest / Her throat burned |
| She felt very happy | Warmth bloomed in her chest / A smile she couldn't fight |
| He was very angry | Fury rolled off him in waves / His knuckles went white |
| She was surprised | The air punched out of her lungs / Her eyes went wide |
| She was scared | Ice crawled up her spine / Her blood ran cold |
| He was very worried | Dread coiled in his gut / His stomach dropped |

### 表情与动作

| 翻译腔 | 地道替代 |
|-------|---------|
| His face was cold | His expression shuttered / Ice settled over his features |
| He frowned | A muscle ticked in his jaw / His brows drew together |
| She bit her lip | She caught her bottom lip between her teeth |
| He smiled | The corner of his mouth ticked up / A dark smile curved his lips |
| He walked in | He filled the doorway / He stepped inside like he owned the place |
| She nodded | She dipped her chin / A barely-there nod |
| He held her hand | His fingers threaded through hers |
| She cried | Tears slipped down her cheeks / Her vision blurred |
| He looked at her deeply | His gaze pinned her in place / His eyes searched hers |
| They looked at each other | Their gazes collided / locked / clashed |

### 描述与氛围

| 翻译腔 | 地道替代 |
|-------|---------|
| It was very quiet | Silence stretched between them like a wire about to snap |
| The atmosphere was tense | The tension in the room could have cracked bone |
| She was very beautiful | The kind of beautiful that made men do stupid things |
| He was tall and handsome | Built like a threat wrapped in a suit |
| The room was big and fancy | Crystal dripped from the ceiling. Money dripped from everything else. |
| Time passed quickly | Hours bled into each other |
| The night was dark | Darkness pressed against the windows like something alive |

### 对话标签

| 翻译腔 | 地道替代 |
|-------|---------|
| He said angrily | He bit out / He growled / He snapped |
| She said softly | She murmured / Her voice barely a whisper |
| He said coldly | His voice could have frozen steel |
| She said happily | A laugh bubbled up her throat |
| He said seriously | His tone left no room for argument |
| She said nervously | Her voice cracked on the last word |

## 附加原则

### 段落长度
英文网文段落通常比中文短得多。一个段落2-4句为宜。大段文字要拆分。单句成段是常见且有效的技巧：

> She ran.

### 句式变化
交替使用长句和短句。短句用于冲击力，长句用于铺陈氛围。

> The door slammed. She flinched. And in the silence that followed—a silence so thick she could taste the dust in it—she heard the unmistakable click of a safety being released.

### 避免过度使用「very」
英文写作中「very + adjective」几乎总是可以用一个更强的词替代：
- very angry → furious / livid
- very sad → devastated / gutted
- very beautiful → stunning / breathtaking
- very scared → terrified / petrified
